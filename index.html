The Retreat

Restore your body and mind balance, with a program of reformer and mat Pilates, yoga and meditation.
Immerse yourself in the relaxing, green surroundings of Ubud, Asia's home for wellness & spirituality.
The perfect setting for exercise and spirituality, led by master teachers Sophia Oh and Emma Jory.

Your teachers

Sophia Oh

Sophia is a certified Pilates and yoga instructor, with 15 years international teaching in Sydney, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur and Oxford. She third level lineage from the founder, Joseph Pilates.
Sophia is currently based in Ubud with her family. She has been returning to the mystical island since her yoga teacher training days back in 2002.

Emma Jory

Emma is a certified Pilates, yoga and Barre Instructor with 13 years teaching experience. She is a certified Health Coach and founded the Emergy Pilates Studio in Sydney.
Emma was the first instructor to teach Barre in Australia. She sold her studio to move to Malaysia, where she is now based with her husband.
